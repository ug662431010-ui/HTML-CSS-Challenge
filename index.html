Hebron is a city in the southern West Bank, Palestine, and the capital of the Hebron Governorate, which is the largest in the West Bank.

Jenin

Jenin is a city in the West Bank, Palestine, and is the capital of the Jenin Governorate.

Jericho

Jericho is a city in the West Bank, Palestine, and the capital of the Jericho Governorate.

Nablus

Nablus has been traditionally associated with the ancient city of Shechem.

Qalqilya

Qalqilyais a city in the West Bank, Palestine, which serves as the administrative center of the Qalqilya Governorate.

Quds (Jerusalem)Quds is the Arabic name for Jerusalem, a holy city for Judaism, Christianity, and Islam.

Ramallah is the administrative capital of Palestine, a city in the central West Bank located north of Jerusalem.

Deir al-Balah is a city in the center of the Gaza Strip, Palestine, and the administrative capital of the Deir al-Balah Governorate.

Gaza

Gaza is the smaller of the two Palestinian territories, the other being the West Bank, that make up the State of Palestine in the Southern Levant region of West Asia.
